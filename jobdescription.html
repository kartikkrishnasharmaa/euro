PRT Computer Teacher - EURO School

Job Title:

PRT Computer Teacher

Location:

Euro International School, Sikar

Salary:

₹25,000 - ₹35,000 per month

Job Type:

Full-time

Responsibilities:

  • Develop and implement lesson plans for primary classes.
  • Teach basic computer skills and software to students.
  • Maintain a safe and conducive learning environment.
